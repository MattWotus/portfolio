About me

Matthew Wotus

Full-Stack Web Developer

Hi! I'm Matthew Wotus, a full-stack developer with an atypical path to the tech industry.

I have a B.A. in Media and Journalism from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill and an M.A. in Interactive Media from Elon University. After graduating from Elon in May 2019, I accepted a job as the Web Designer at Meredith College, where my primary responsibility was creating and implementing desktop and mobile designs on new and existing webpages on the College's website.

After eight months at Meredith, I decided to resign my position and enroll in the Full Immersion, Full-Stack Web Development Bootcamp at LearningFuze, a 14-week accelerated program where I'm learning front-end and back-end development, to learn the skills necessary to achieve my goal of being a professional web developer.

In the program, I am refreshing my knowledge of HTML5 and CSS3 from graduate school, as well as learning JavaScript (ES5/ES6), React, jQuery, Ajax, PostgreSQL and Node.js.
